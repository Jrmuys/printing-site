About ThriftyPrint

The Thrifty Bot owns and operates multiple Markforged Onyx One desktop 3D printers. This allows us to create parts made from Markforged Onyx. Onyx has a flexural strength of 81 MPa (11.7 ksi) and is very robust. Onyx has an extraordinarily high strength to weight ratio and an ultra smooth surface finish, which makes it ideal for competitive robotics. For more information on Onyx, see the manufacturer’s data sheet.

We have made several parts for teams and companies alike, and this website’s goal is to make that process as streamlined as possible. We aim for the best possible customer experience with every transaction. If you have ideas to make the site better, please contact us at contact@thethriftybot.com.
